Welcome to my site about roller coasters!

Since I was young I have loved going to amusement parks and in particular riding roller coasters. I live in the Detroit metro area so I am only a 2 hour drive from Cedar Point in Sandusky, Ohio so I visit there at least a couple of times per year. I've been to a few other parks including Kings Dominion in Doswell, Virginia, as well as Universal Studios and Islands of Adventure in Orlando, Florida, and Busch Gardens in Tampa Bay, Florida. Here is a collection of some of my favorite coasters I've ridden.
